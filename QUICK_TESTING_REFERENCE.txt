⚠️ COMMON ERRORS & FIXES

   ERROR: "Project is paused due to inactivity"
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────
   FIX: Restore Appwrite project
        1. https://sgp.cloud.appwrite.io/console
        2. Click "Restore" button
        3. Wait 30 seconds
        4. Retry payment


   ERROR: "Full name is required" (Form doesn't submit)
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   FIX: Enter valid data
        - Full Name: at least 2 characters
        - Mobile: 11 digits starting with 01
        - Address: at least 5 characters
        - Select both District and Thana


   ERROR: Form won't load or blank page
   ────────────────────────────────────
   FIX: Clear browser cache
        1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (or Cmd+Shift+Delete on Mac)
        2. Check "Cookies and cached images and files"
        3. Click "Delete data"
        4. Refresh page


   ERROR: "Cannot connect to payment gateway"
   ────────────────────────────────────────
   FIX: Check internet connection
        1. Verify you can access: https://www.sslcommerz.com
        2. Try in different browser
        3. Try in private/incognito window
        4. Restart browser
