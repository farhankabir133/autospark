- Select "Continue with GitHub"
- Authorize Vercel

- Click "New Project"
- Select `autospark` repo
- Click "Import"

**Step 3**: Configure
- Root Directory: `next-sslcommerz`
- Add 6 environment variables (copy from guide)

**Step 4**: Deploy
- Click "Deploy"
- Wait 1-2 minutes
- Get your URL!
